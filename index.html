How does it work?

This Friend Finder gets a list of all WordCamps and publicly listed[1] attendees which you can then search right in your browser[2].

It can do so in a simple 2-step process:

  1. Pick all WordCamps you are interested in. All attendees will then be loaded.
  2. In the result you can search by (partial) name or full email[3].
WordCamp Friend Finder needs JavaScript to work.
🚀 Get started

Notes

[1] When buying a ticket for a WordCamp each attendee can choose to not be publicly listed in which case the WordCamp Friend Finder is obviously unable to find them.
[2] This means that any names or email adresses you enter never leave your device for maximum privacy.
[3] If you wonder how searching for email works: Gravatar does expose MD5 hashes of email addresses which makes it trivial to find a user by email if, and only if, you already know the email address. For details have a look at Wikipedia and the linked sources there.
